Mario Kart Wii

Mario Kart Wii (2008) is one of the best and, most broken Mario Karts ever. The physics of the game, shortcuts, ultrashortcuts, and CTGP has really enganced and kept this game alive for over a decade.

Between the modding comunity and a very knowlegable MrBean3500vr has kept this game enjoyable and neverending. There will always be 1 in a million moments, and part of that is just the games nature and reputation.

Shortcuts.

In the base game, (All 32 of nintendo's tracks) there are tons of involving shortcuts that take practice and skill to complete. Examples include the DKS double, Toad's factory lake cut, Mushroom gorges gap jump, Wii Rainbow roads Moonjump, and of course the Grumble volcano ultra shortcut. Each one is unique in terms of both inputs, and eficency. The Wii RR moonjump is just a wheelie, and a shroom boost, and the TF lake cut is a delay trick, hop trick, and then you snape the joystick left, and you get air. And the GV ultra shortcut is infamos for driving aroun a rock 3 times.

As good as the Custom Tracks are, The Vanilla shortcuts inspiered 100's of CT creators (Custom Tracks) to impliment these ideas into their own tracks. As challenging, easy, and diverse as each and every one is, the good ones share similar track elements to those Nintendo Made.
